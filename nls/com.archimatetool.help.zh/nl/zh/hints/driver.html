A driver is defined as something that creates, motivates, and fuels the change in an organization.

Drivers may be internal, in which case they are usually associated with a stakeholder. They may also be external.

The name of a driver should preferably be a noun.

Category:

Motivation

Examples:

"Customer satisfaction", "Compliance to legislation", "Profitability", "Economic changes", "Changing legislation"

Typical Relationships:

Composition, Aggregation, Specialisation, Influence
