A location is defined as a conceptual point or extent in space.

The location concept is used to model the distribution of structural elements such as business actors, application components, and devices. This is modelled by means of an assignment relationship from location to structural element. Indirectly, a location can also be assigned to a behaviour element, to indicate where the behaviour is performed.

Category:

Business. Active Structure.

Examples:

Main Office, Local Office, Room.

Typical Relationships:

Assignment - business role, business actor
Aggregation - business actor
